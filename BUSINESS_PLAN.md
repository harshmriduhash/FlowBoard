## üéØ Problem

### The Core Problem

**Workflow management today is broken:**

1. **Manual Overload**
   - Teams spend 10-20 hours/week updating boards manually
   - Status checks require constant human intervention
   - No proactive alerts when things go wrong

2. **Visibility Gap**
   - Hidden blockers until it's too late
   - Managers ask "what's the status?" repeatedly
   - Work gets stuck with no visibility

3. **Static Tools**
   - Trello/Asana/Monday are digital spreadsheets
   - No intelligence, just data storage
   - User has to DO everything

4. **No Natural Interface**
   - Always typing, clicking, navigating
   - Not accessible when hands-free needed
   - Slow to get answers

### Who Feels This Pain?

**Primary:**
- Project Managers managing 5-20 people
- Engineering leads coordinating technical work
- Operations teams running processes
- Startup founders wearing many hats

**Secondary:**
- Freelancers managing client work
- Consultants tracking deliverables
- Small agencies (5-20 people)

### Current Alternatives (And Why They Fail)

| Tool | Weakness |
|------|----------|
| Trello | No AI, manual only, no workflow validation |
| Asana | Complex, expensive, still manual |
| Monday.com | Expensive, overwhelming UI |
| Notion | Not workflow-focused, no agents |
| Linear | Eng-only, no AI assistance |

**Gap:** None have AI agents or voice interface.

## üö® Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: OpenAI Dependency
**Risk:** Dependent on OpenAI API pricing/availability  
**Mitigation:**
- Build abstraction layer for multiple providers
- Monitor usage and optimize prompts
- Consider self-hosted models for high-volume users
- Cache common responses

### Risk 2: Competition
**Risk:** Existing players (Trello, Asana) add AI  
**Mitigation:**
- Move fast, iterate faster
- Build community moat
- Focus on voice (harder to copy)
- Continuous innovation

### Risk 3: Low Conversion
**Risk:** Free users don't convert to paid  
**Mitigation:**
- Implement smart upgrade prompts
- Reduce free tier limits over time
- Improve onboarding for value demonstration
- A/B test pricing

### Risk 4: Slow Growth
**Risk:** User acquisition slower than projected  
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify channels
- Increase content output
- Partnerships and integrations
- Small paid acquisition budget
