## ðŸš¨ Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Conversion Rate
**Risk:** Free users don't convert to paid  
**Mitigation:** 
- Strong onboarding flow showing AI value
- Usage limits on free tier
- In-app upgrade prompts at friction points

### Risk 2: High OpenAI Costs
**Risk:** AI costs spiral as usage grows  
**Mitigation:**
- Implement response caching
- Rate limiting per tier
- Monitor usage and optimize prompts
- Consider fine-tuned models for common queries

### Risk 3: Competition
**Risk:** Competitors copy AI features  
**Mitigation:**
- Move fast, build brand loyalty
- Continuous innovation in AI agents
- Focus on integration quality
- Build community

### Risk 4: Churn
**Risk:** Customers cancel after trial  
**Mitigation:**
- Strong customer onboarding
- Regular feature updates
- Proactive support
- Usage analytics to prevent churn
